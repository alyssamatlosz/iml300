Alyssa Matlosz - Week 5 - The good, the bad, the ugly

The Good, The Bad, The Ugly

1. Good Layout

I chose buzzfeed as the good layout example because it is very functional. It includes a lot of information on one page without feeling overwhelming or overcrowded. The nav bar is simple, with an exploratory header section. The body is clearly divided into 3 columns to divide the page's information, which becomes more noticeable as you scroll. It does not take up the full page with, which creates the image of spaciousness even with a condensed amount of space with actual content.

2. Bad Layout

This astrology site does not seem very purposefully formatted. The wide central column feels interrupted by the right column, which does not continue down the entire page which adds more awkwardness as you scroll down. The navigation bar is not too bad, but the side bar is clunky and it overall feels very unbalanced with the amount of poorly spaced text.
